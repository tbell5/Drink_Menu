1 ounce butterscotch schnapps
1 ounce Jägermeister
1 ounce Baileys Irish Cream
¼ ounce pastis
½ ounce heavy cream
Freshly grated or ground nutmeg

Shake all liquid ingredients but the cream vigorously with ice. Strain into an 
ice-filled old-fashioned glass. Float the cream on top, and dust with nutmeg.

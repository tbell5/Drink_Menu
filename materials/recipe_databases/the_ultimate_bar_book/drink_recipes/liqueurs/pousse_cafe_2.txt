¼ ounce raspberry syrup
¼ ounce anisette
¼ ounce Parfait Amour
¼ ounce crème de violette
¼ ounce yellow Chartreause
¼ ounce green Chartreuse
¼ ounce brandy

Pour the first liqueur into a pousse-café glass or sherry glass. Slowly and carefully layer the other ingredients, one on top of the other, in the order given.

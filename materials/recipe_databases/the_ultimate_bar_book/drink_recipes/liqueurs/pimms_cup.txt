3 ounces Pimm's No. 1
3 to 4 ounces chilled club soda
Cucumber spear
Lemon slice

Pour the Pimm's No. 1 into an ice-filled highball glass. Top with club soda and 
stir gently. Garnish with the cucumber spear and lemon slice.

VARIATION: Instead of the club soda, top with a splash of ginger ale, lemonade, 
or lemon-lime soda.

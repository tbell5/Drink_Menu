1 ounce dark rum 
1 ounce light rum 
¼ ounce Tia Maria 
1¼ ounces fresh orange juice 
1 ounce fresh lime juice 
Fresh mint sprig 
Maraschino cherry 

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into a chilled 
highball glass. Garnish with the mint sprig and cherry. VARIATION: Substitute ½ 
ounce Galliano and ½ ounce Cointreau for the Tia Maria and orange juice.

1 1/2 ounces dark rum 
1 ounce light rum 
1/2 ounce passion fruit juice 
1 1/2 ounces fresh orange juice 
1 ounce fresh lime juice 
1 ounce pineapple juice 
Dash of Angostura bitters 
Lime wedge 
Pineapple wedge 

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into an ice-filled 
wineglass. Garnish with the lime and pineapple wedges.

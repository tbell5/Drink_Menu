1 ounce dark rum 
1/2 ounce coconut rum 
1/2 ounce coffee liqueur 
4 ounces pineapple juice 
1 1/2 ounces fresh lemon juice 
1 tablespoon 151-proof Demerara rum 
Pineapple wedge 
Maraschino cherry 

Shake all liquid ingredients but the 151 rum vigorously with ice. Strain into a 
chilled cocktail glass. Float the 151 on top of the drink. Garnish with the 
pineapple wedge and cherry.

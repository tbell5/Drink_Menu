1 1/2 ounces Malibu rum 
1 ounce Thai coconut milk 
1/4 ounce Goldschläger cinnamon schnapps 
Ground cinnamon 
orange blossom 

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into a chilled 
cocktail glass. Sprinkle a dusting of cinnamon over the top, and float the 
orange blossom on top. 

VARIATION: For those who are fond of cinnamon, add an additional ¼ ounce of the 
cinnamon schnapps.

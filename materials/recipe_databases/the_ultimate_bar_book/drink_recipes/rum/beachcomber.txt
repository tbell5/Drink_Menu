Lime wedge 
Superfine sugar 
2 ounces gold rum 
1/2 ounce Cointreau 
1/4 ounce maraschino liqueur 
1/4 ounce cherry brandy 
1/2 ounce fresh lime juice 
Lime slice 

Rub the rim of a chilled wineglass with the lime wedge and rim with sugar. 
Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into the prepared 
glass. Garnish with the lime slice.

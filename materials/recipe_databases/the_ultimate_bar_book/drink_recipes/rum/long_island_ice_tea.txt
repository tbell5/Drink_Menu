3/4 ounce white rum 
3/4 ounce gin 
3/4 ounce vodka 
3/4 ounce tequila 
3/4 ounce Cointreau or triple sec 
1 ounce fresh lime juice 
3/4 ounce fresh orange juice 
2 to 3 ounces chilled cola 
Lemon wedge 

Pour all the liquid ingredients except the cola into an ice-filled collins 
glass. Top with cola and stir gently. Squeeze the lemon wedge over the drink, and drop it in.

3 ounces blanco rum 
1/2 ounce maraschino liqueur 
1 1/2 ounces fresh grapefruit juice 
3/4 ounce fresh lime juice 
Lime wedge 

Combine all ingredients but the garnish in a blender with 1/2 cup ice. Blend 
until smooth. Pour into a chilled cocktail glass. Squeeze the lime wedge over 
the drink, and drop it in.

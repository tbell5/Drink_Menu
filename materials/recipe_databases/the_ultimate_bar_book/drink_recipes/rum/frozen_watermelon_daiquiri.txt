1 cup seeded, cubed watermelon 
1 1/2 ounces white rum 
3/4 ounce fresh lime juice 
1/4 cup lime sorbet 
Lime wheel 

In a blender, purée the watermelon until completely smooth. Add the rum, lime 
juice, lime sorbet, and ½ cup ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into a chilled 
cocktail glass. Garnish with the lime wheel.

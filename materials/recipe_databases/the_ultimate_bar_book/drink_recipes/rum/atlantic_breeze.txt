1 1/2 ounces light rum 
1/2 ounce apricot liqueur 
2 1/2 ounces pineapple juice 
1/2 ounce fresh lemon juice 
1/4 ounce Galliano 
Orange slice 

Pour all the liquid ingredients except the Galliano into an ice-filled highball 
glass. Stir briefly. Float the Galliano on top of the drink. Garnish with the 
orange slice.

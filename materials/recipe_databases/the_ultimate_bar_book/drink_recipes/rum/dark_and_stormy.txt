1 to 2 slices fresh ginger (optional) 
2 ounces dark rum 
1/2 tablespoon ginger syrup or simple syrup 
3 to 4 ounces chilled ginger beer 
2 lime wedges 

Fill an old-fashioned glass with ice and add the fresh ginger. Add the rum and 
ginger syrup, and stir. Top with ginger beer. Squeeze the lime wedges over the 
drink and drop them in.

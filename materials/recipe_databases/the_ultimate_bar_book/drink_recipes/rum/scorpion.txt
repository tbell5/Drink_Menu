2 ounces light or gold rum 
1/2 ounce brandy 
1/2 ounce dry vermouth 
1 1/2 ounces fresh orange juice 
1 1/2 ounces fresh lemon juice 
1/2 ounce orgeat syrup 
Pineapple spear 
Maraschino cherry 
Edible flower (such as orange blossom, gardenia, or nasturtium) 

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into a large ice- 
filled wineglass. Garnish with the skewered pineapple and cherry, and float the 
flower on top.

1 lemon wedge 
2 lime wedges 
2 tangerine (or orange) wedges 
1 tablespoon sugar 
2 ounces Rhum Barbancourt 
3 to 4 ounces chilled ginger beer (or ginger ale) 

Muddle the lemon, lime, and orange wedges with the sugar in the bottom of an 
old-fashioned glass. Fill the glass with ice, pour in the rum, and top with 
ginger beer.

2 ounces dark rum Dash of Cointreau 
1 1/2 ounces fresh orange juice 
1 1/2 ounces pineapple juice 
1/2 ounces fresh lime juice 
1/2 ounce simple syrup 
Dash of grenadine 
Orange slice 
Maraschino cherry 

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into an ice-filled 
highball glass. Garnish with the orange slice and cherry.

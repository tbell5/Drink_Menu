1 1/2 ounces Myers’s dark rum 
1 ounce light rum 
1 ounce Frangelico 
1/2 ounce Kahlúa 
3 ounces pineapple juice 
1 ounce fresh lime juice 
1 tablespoon simple syrup 
Lime wedge 

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into a chilled large 
collins glass. Squeeze the lime wedge over the drink, and drop it in.

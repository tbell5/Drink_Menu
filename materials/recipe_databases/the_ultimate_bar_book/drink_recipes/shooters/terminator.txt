½ ounce Kahlúa (or Tia Maria)
½ ounce Irish cream liqueur
½ ounce sambuca
½ ounce Grand Marnier
½ ounce vodka

Pour the Kahlúa into a pousse-café glass or sherry glass. Slowly layer the 
other ingredients, one on top of the other, in the order given, pouring them 
over the back of a spoon. Do not mix. Drink in one gulp.

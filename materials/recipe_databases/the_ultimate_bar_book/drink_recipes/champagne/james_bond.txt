1 sugar cube
Dash of Angostura bitters
¾ ounce vodka
¼ ounce Lillet
3 to 5 ounces chilled champagne
Lemon twist

In the bottom of a chilled champagne flute, soak the sugar cube with the 
bitters. Pour in the vodka and Lillet. Slowly top with champagne. Run the lemon 
peel around the rim, twist it over the drink, and drop it in.

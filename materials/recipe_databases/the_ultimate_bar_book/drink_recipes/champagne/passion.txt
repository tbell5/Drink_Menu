1 ounce aged Bacardi (8 years old)
½ ounce crème de pêche
1 ounce fresh lime juice
½ ounce simple syrup
1 ounce passion fruit purée
3 to 5 ounces chilled champagne

Shake all ingredients but the champagne vigorously with ice. Strain into an 
ice-filled highball glass, and slowly top with champagne.

1½ ounces cranberry nectar
½ ounce pear brandy
½ ounce Cointreau
3 to 4 ounces chilled champagne 
2 fresh cranberries

Shake the cranberry nectar, pear brandy, and Cointreau vigorously with ice. 
Strain into a chilled champagne flute. Slowly top with the champagne, and 
garnish with the cranberries.

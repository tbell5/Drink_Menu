1½ ounces good-quality rum
¾ ounce fresh lime juice
Dash of Angostura bitters
1 ounce simple syrup
6 fresh mint leaves
1½ ounces chilled champagne
Half a vanilla bean

Shake the rum, lime juice, bitters, simple syrup, and mint vigorously with ice. 
Strain into a chilled champagne flute. Slowly top with champagne, and stir 
gently. Garnish with the vanilla bean.

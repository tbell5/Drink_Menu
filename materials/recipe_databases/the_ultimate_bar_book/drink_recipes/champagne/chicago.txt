Lemon wedge
Superfine sugar
1½ ounces brandy
¼ teaspoon Cointreau
Dash of Angostura bitters
3 to 5 ounces chilled champagne

Rub the rim of a chilled wineglass with the lemon wedge, and rim with sugar. 
Shake all ingredients but the champagne vigorously with ice. Strain into the 
prepared glass, and slowly top with champagne.

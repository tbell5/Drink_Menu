3 ounces bourbon (or blended whiskey) 
1 ounce cranberry juice 
½ ounce fresh lemon juice 
1 ounce simple syrup 
Lemon slice 

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into an ice-filled 
old-fashioned glass. Garnish with the lemon slice. 

VARIATION: Combine the liquid ingredients in a blender with ½ cup ice. Blend 
for a few seconds, and pour into a chilled wineglass.

2 ounces Irish whiskey 
¼ ounce Cointreau 
¼ ounce absinthe substitute (Pernod or anisette) 
Dash of maraschino liqueur 
Dash of Angostura bitters 
Lemon twist 

Stir the liquid ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Strain into a chilled 
cocktail glass. Run the lemon peel around the rim, twist it over the drink, and 
drop it in.

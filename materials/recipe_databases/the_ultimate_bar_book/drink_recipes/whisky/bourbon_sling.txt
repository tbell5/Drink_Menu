2½ ounces bourbon 
½ ounce Southern Comfort 
½ ounce fresh lemon juice 
5 to 6 ounces chilled club soda 
Lemon wedge 

Shake all liquid ingredients except the club soda vigorously with ice. Strain 
into an ice-filled highball glass. Top with club soda and stir gently. Squeeze 
the lemon wedge over the drink, and drop it in.

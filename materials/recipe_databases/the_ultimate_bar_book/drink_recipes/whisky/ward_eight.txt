2 ounces bourbon (or rye) 
1 ounce fresh lemon juice 
1 ounce fresh orange juice 
Dash of grenadine 
Maraschino cherry 
Orange slice 
Lemon slice 

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into an ice-filled 
old-fashioned glass or a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with the cherry and 
orange and lemon slices.

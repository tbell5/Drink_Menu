1 sugar cube Dash of Peychaud’s or Angostura bitters 
2 ounces bourbon 
¼ ounce Herbsaint (or Pernod or Ricard) 
½ ounce fresh lemon juice 
Lemon twist 

Place the sugar cube in the bottom of an old-fashioned glass, and saturate with 
the bitters. Add ice and pour in the bourbon, Herb- saint, and lemon juice. 
Stir well. Twist the lemon peel over the drink, and drop it in.

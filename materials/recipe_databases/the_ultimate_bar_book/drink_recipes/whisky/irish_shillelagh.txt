1½ ounces Irish whiskey 
½ ounce light rum 
½ ounce sloe gin 
1 ounce fresh lemon juice 
1 teaspoon sugar 
Peach slice 
Orange slice 
2 to 3 raspberries 
Maraschino cherry 

Shake all the ingredients except for the fruit vigorously with ice. Strain into 
an ice-filled old-fashioned glass. Garnish with the fruit.

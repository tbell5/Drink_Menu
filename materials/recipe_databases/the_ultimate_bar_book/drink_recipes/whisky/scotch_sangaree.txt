2 ounces scotch 
½ ounce port 
½ ounce Drambuie 
Freshly grated or ground nutmeg 

Stir the liquid ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Strain into a chilled 
wineglass. Sprinkle the top with a dusting of nutmeg. 

VARIATION: Add a teaspoon of heather honey (with a little bit of water) and top 
with club soda.

Lemon twist 
2 ounces Canadian blended whisky 
½ ounce Bénédictine 
½ ounce sweet vermouth 
2 dashes Angostura bitters 

Rub the lemon peel around the rim of a chilled cocktail glass. Shake the liquid 
ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into the prepared glass. Twist the 
lemon peel over the drink, and drop it in.

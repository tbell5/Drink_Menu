2 ounces bourbon 
½ ounce dark crème de cacao 
4 ounces milk (or 3 ounces half-and-half) 
Dash of vanilla extract 
¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon 
Freshly grated or ground nutmeg 

Shake the liquid ingredients and cinnamon vigorously with ice. Strain into an 
ice-filled old-fashioned glass. Sprinkle the top with nutmeg.

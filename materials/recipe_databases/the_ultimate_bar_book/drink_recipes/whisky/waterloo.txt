1½ ounces blended whiskey 
¾ ounce Mandarine Napoléon liqueur 
¼ ounce fresh lemon juice 
¼ ounce simple syrup 
3 to 5 ounces chilled club soda 
Orange slice 

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into an ice-filled 
old-fashioned glass. Top with club soda and stir gently. Garnish with the 
orange slice.

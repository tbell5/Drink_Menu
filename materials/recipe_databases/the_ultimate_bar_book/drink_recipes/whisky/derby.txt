1½ ounces blended whiskey 
½ ounce sweet vermouth 
½ ounce Cointreau 
½ ounce fresh lime juice 

Mint sprig Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into an 
ice-filled old-fashioned glass. Garnish with the mint sprig. 

VARIATION: For an Oriental Cocktail, substitute bourbon for the blended 
whiskey, and omit the mint sprig.

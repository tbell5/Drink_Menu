Lemon wedge 
2 ounces bourbon 
5 ounces chilled ginger ale 

Squeeze the lemon wedge into an ice-filled highball glass. Add the bourbon, top 
with ginger ale, and stir gently. 

VARIATIONS: 
For a Scotch Buck, substitute scotch for the bourbon. 

For an Irish Buck, substitute Irish whiskey for the bourbon.

Lemon wedge 
Sugar 
Lemon peel spiral 
2 ounces bourbon 
½ ounce Cointreau (or maraschino liqueur) 
½ ounce fresh lemon juice 

Rub the rim of a chilled sour glass with the lemon wedge and rim with sugar. 
Uncoil the lemon peel spiral in the prepared glass. Shake the liquid 
ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into the prepared glass. 

VARIATION: For a Scotch Crusta, substitute scotch for the bourbon and 
maraschino liqueur for the Cointreau.

2 ounces blended or other whiskey 
½ ounce fresh lime juice 
¼ ounce simple syrup 
3 to 5 ounces chilled club soda 
Lime wedge 

Shake the whiskey, lime juice, and simple syrup vigorously with ice. Strain 
into an ice-filled highball glass. Top with club soda and stir gently. Squeeze 
the lime wedge over the drink, and drop it in.

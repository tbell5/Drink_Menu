Lemon wedge
Powdered sugar
1½ ounces citron vodka
½ ounce good-quality sake
1 ounce fresh lemon juice
½ tablespoon ginger syrup (page 73)
Slice of candied ginger

Rub the rim of a chilled cocktail glass with the lemon wedge and rim with 
powdered sugar. Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into 
the prepared glass and garnish with the candied ginger.

4 ounces chilled sake
½ ounce high-quality gin (such as Bombay Sapphire)
½ teaspoon dry vermouth
Lemon twist

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into a chilled 
cocktail glass. Twist the lemon peel over the drink, and drop it in.

VARIATION: Substitute ½ ounce of dry sherry for the gin.

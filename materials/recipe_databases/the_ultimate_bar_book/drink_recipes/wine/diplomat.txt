1½ ounces dry vermouth
½ ounce sweet vermouth
½ teaspoon maraschino liqueur
2 dashes Angostura bitters
Lemon twist
Maraschino cherry

Stir the liquid ingredients in a mixing glass with ice. Strain into a chilled 
cocktail glass. Run the lemon peel around the rim, twist it over the drink, and 
drop it in. Garnish with the cherry.

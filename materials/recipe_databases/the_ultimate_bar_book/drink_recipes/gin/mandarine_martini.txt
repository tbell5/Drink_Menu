¼ ounce Mandarine Napoléon
Dash of Cointreau
1½ ounces gin
½ ounce vodka
Mandarin or orange peel spiral

Pour the Mandarine Napoléon and the Cointreau into a chilled cocktail glass, 
swirl to coat the inside of the glass, and discard. Shake the gin and vodka 
vigorously with ice. Strain into the prepared glass, and garnish with the 
mandarin peel spiral.

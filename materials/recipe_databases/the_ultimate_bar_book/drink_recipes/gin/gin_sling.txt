2 ounces gin
½ ounce Cointreau
½ ounce fresh lemon juice
3 to 5 ounces chilled club soda
Lemon wedge

Shake the gin, Cointreau, and lemon juice vigorously with ice. Strain into an 
ice-filled highball glass. Top with club soda and stir gently. Squeeze the 
lemon wedge over the drink, and drop it in.

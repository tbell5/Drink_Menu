4 to 5 fresh blackberries
1 teaspoon sugar
1 ounce fresh lime juice
1½ ounces gin
½ ounce crème de mûre (blackberry liqueur)
3 to 4 ounces chilled club soda
Lime wedge

In the bottom of a chilled highball glass, muddle together the blackberries, 
sugar, and lime juice. Fill the glass with ice and add the gin and crème de 
mûre. Top with club soda and stir briefly. Garnish with the lime wedge.

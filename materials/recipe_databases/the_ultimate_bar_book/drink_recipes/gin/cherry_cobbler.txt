2 ounces gin
¾ ounce Cherry Heering
¼ ounce crème de cassis
¼ ounce fresh lemon juice
¼ ounce simple syrup
2 to 3 ounces chilled club soda (optional)
Lemon slice
Maraschino cherry
Fresh mint sprig

Shake all the liquid ingredients except the club soda vigorously with ice. 
Strain into a chilled old-fashioned glass. Top with club soda, if using. 
Garnish with the lemon slice, cherry, and mint sprig.

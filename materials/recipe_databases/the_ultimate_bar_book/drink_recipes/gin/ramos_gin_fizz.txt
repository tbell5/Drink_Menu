1 ounce fresh lemon juice
½ ounce fresh lime juice
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
2 to 3 dashes orange flower water
1 egg white (optional)
1½ ounces heavy cream
1 tablespoon superfine sugar
2 to 4 ounces chilled club soda
Lemon slice

Shake all the ingredients except the club soda and lemon slice vigorously with 
ice for 3 to 4 minutes. Strain into a chilled highball glass. Top with club 
soda and stir gently. Garnish with the lemon slice.

1½ ounces gin
2 ounces guava nectar
½ ounce fresh lime juice
1 ounce pomegranate syrup
3 to 4 ounces chilled club soda
Lime wheel
Orange blossom (or other edible flower)

Shake the gin, guava nectar, lime juice, and pomegranate syrup vigorously with 
ice. Strain into an ice-filled highball glass. Top with club soda. Garnish with 
the lime wheel and orange blossom.

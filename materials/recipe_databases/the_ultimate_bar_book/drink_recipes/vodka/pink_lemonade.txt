1½ ounces vodka
½ ounce triple sec
1 ounce cranberry juice
½ ounce fresh lemon juice
½ ounce fresh lime juice
4 to 5 ounces chilled 7-Up
Lemon wedge

Shake all liquid ingredients except the 7-Up vigorously with ice. Strain into 
an ice-filled collins glass. Top with 7-Up and stir gently. Squeeze the lemon 
wedge over the drink, and drop it in.

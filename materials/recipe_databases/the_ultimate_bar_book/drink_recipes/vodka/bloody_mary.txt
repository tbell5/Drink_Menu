2 ounces vodka

4 ounces tomato juice
½ ounce fresh lemon juice
¼ teaspoon horseradish (freshly grated, if possible) 
2 to 3 dashes Tabasco sauce
2 to 3 dashes Worcestershire sauce
Lemon wedge
Celery stick

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into an ice- filled 
highball glass. Squeeze the lemon wedge over the drink, and drop it in. Garnish 
with the celery stick.

VARIATIONS: 
For a Caesar (also known as Bloody Caesar or Clamdigger), substitute Clamato 
juice for the tomato juice.

For a Bloody Marie, add ½ teaspoon anisette.

For a Virgin Mary, omit the vodka.

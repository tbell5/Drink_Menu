1½ ounces vodka
½ ounce Tuaca
½ ounce white crème de cacao
2 to 3 ounces sparkling tangerine or orange beverage
Orange slice
Fresh mint sprig

Shake the vodka, Tuaca, and crème de cacao vigorously with ice. Strain into an 
ice-filled highball glass. Top with the sparkling tangerine beverage. Garnish 
with the orange slice and mint sprig.

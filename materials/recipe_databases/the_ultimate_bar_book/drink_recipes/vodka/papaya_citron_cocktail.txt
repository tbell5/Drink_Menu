1½ ounces citron vodka
4 ounces passion fruit nectar
1 ounce fresh lime juice
1½ cups seeded, peeled, cubed papaya
8 fresh mint leaves
Lime wheel
Papaya slice
Fresh mint sprig

Combine all ingredients but the garnishes in a blender. Blend until smooth. 
Pour into an ice-filled highball glass. Garnish with a skewer of lime wheel, 
papaya slice, and mint sprig.

VARIATION: For a great nonalcoholic smoothie, simply omit the vodka.

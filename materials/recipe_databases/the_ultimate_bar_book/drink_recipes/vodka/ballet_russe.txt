1 ounce vodka
¾ ounce crème de cassis
1½ ounces fresh lime juice
½ ounce fresh lemon juice
1 ounce simple syrup

Shake the ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into a chilled cocktail 
glass.

VARIATION: For a Russian Fizz, add 3 to 5 ounces ginger ale and stir. Serve 
over ice in a highball glass.

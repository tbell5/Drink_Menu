2 ounces black currant vodka
¾ ounce Cointreau
¾ ounce cranberry juice
¾ ounce fresh lime juice
Thinly sliced lime wheel or flamed orange twist

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice, and strain into a chilled 
cocktail glass. Float the lime wheel on top of the drink, or garnish with a 
flamed orange peel.

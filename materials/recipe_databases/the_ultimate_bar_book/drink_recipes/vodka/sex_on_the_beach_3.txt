1 ounce vodka
½ ounce Chambord
½ ounce melon liqueur
1 ounce pineapple juice
¼ ounce cranberry juice

Shake all ingredients except the cranberry juice vigorously with ice. Strain 
into a chilled cocktail glass (or, if you prefer, over ice in a highball 
glass). Float the cranberry juice on top of the drink.

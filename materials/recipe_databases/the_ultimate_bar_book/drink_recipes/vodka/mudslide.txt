1 ounce vodka
1 ounce Kahlúa
1 ounce Baileys Irish Cream
1 ounce heavy cream
Unsweetened cocoa powder (or chocolate shavings)

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into a chilled 
cocktail glass. Sprinkle the top with cocoa powder or chocolate shavings.

VARIATION: For a Frozen Mudslide, combine the ingredients in a blender with ½ 
cup crushed ice. Blend until smooth. Pour into a chilled wineglass.

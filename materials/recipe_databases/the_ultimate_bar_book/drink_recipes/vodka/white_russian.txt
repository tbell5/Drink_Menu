2 ounces vodka
1 ounce Kahlúa
1 ounce heavy cream

OVER ICE: Pour the vodka and Kahlúa into an ice-filled old-fashioned glass, and 
stir. Float the cream on top.

UP: Stir the vodka and Kahlúa in a mixing glass with ice. Strain into a chilled 
sherry glass. Float the cream on top.

BLENDED (KNOWN AS A RUSSIAN COFFEE): Combine the ingredients in a blender with 
½ cup crushed ice. Blend until smooth. Serve in a chilled cocktail glass.

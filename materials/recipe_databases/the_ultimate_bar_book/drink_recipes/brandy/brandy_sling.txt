2½ ounces brandy
½ ounce Grand Marnier
½ ounce fresh lemon juice
2 to 4 ounces chilled club soda
Lemon wedge

Shake the brandy, Grand Marnier, and lemon juice vigorously with ice. Strain 
into an ice-filled highball glass. Top with club soda and stir gently. Squeeze 
the lemon wedge over the drink, and drop it in.

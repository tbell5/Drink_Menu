2 ounces brandy
¾ ounce fresh orange juice
½ ounce simple syrup
Maraschino cherry
Orange slice

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into a chilled sour 
glass. Garnish with the cherry and orange slice.

VARIATIONS: 
For a lemon-lime version, use ½ ounce lime juice and ½ ounce lemon juice in 
place of the orange juice.

For an Apple Brandy Sour, substitute Calvados or applejack for the brandy, and 
add a dash of grenadine.

For a Fireman's Sour, add 1 ounce grenadine.

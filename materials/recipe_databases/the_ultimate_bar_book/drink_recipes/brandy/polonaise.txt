1½ ounces brandy
½ ounce blackberry brandy
½ ounce dry sherry
Dash of fresh lemon juice
Dash of orange bitters

Shake the ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into an ice-filled old-
fashioned glass.

VARIATION: For a Poop Deck Cocktail, substitute 1 ounce ruby port for the 
sherry. Shake and strain into a chilled cocktail glass.

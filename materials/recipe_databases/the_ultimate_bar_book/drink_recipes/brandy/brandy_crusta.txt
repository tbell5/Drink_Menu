Lemon wedge
Superfine sugar
2 ounces brandy
½ ounce Cointreau
½ ounce fresh lemon juice
Dash of maraschino liqueur
Lemon peel spiral

Rub the rim of a chilled sour glass with the lemon wedge and rim with sugar. 
Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into the prepared 
glass. Garnish with the lemon spiral.

1 ripe nectarine, pitted and sliced
2 ounces reposado tequila
1 ounce Mandarine Napolean liqueur
1 1/2 ounces fresh lime juice
1/2 ounce fresh orange juice
1 tablespoon superfine sugar
Nectarine slice

Combine all ingredients but the garnish in a blender with 1 cup cracked ice. 
Blend until smooth. Pour into a chilled wineglass. Garnish with the nectarine 
slice.

2 cups cubed ripe papaya
6 ounces fresh sweet-and-sour
6 ounces silver tequila
2 ounces Damiana liqueur
4 lime wedges
4 small pink orchids (optional)

Makes 4 servings. Chill 4 margarita glasses. Set aside. In a blender, mix the
papaya, sweet-and-sour, tequila, and Damiana until thoroughly pureed. Add 2
cups ice and blend until the mixture is smooth. Divide the mixture evenly among
the prepared glasses. Garnish each with a skewered lime wedge and orchid.

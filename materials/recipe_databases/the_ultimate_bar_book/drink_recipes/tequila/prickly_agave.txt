Lime wedge
Sugar and salt
1 1/2 onuces silver tequila
1 ounce Tuaca
1 ounce prickly pear juice
1 ounce fresh lime juice
Lime peel spiral

Rub the rim of a chilled margarita or large cocktail glass with the lime wedge 
and rim with sugar and salt. Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice.
Strain into the prepared glass, and garnish with the lime peel spiral.

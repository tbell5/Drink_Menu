1 1/2 ounces silver tequila
2 ounces tomato juice
1 ounce fresh orange juice
1 teaspoon honey
1/2 ounce fresh lime juice
1 tablespoon diced onion
1 teaspoon diced jalepeno chile
2 to 3 dashes Worcestershire sauce
Lime wedge
Jalapeno chile slice

Shake all ingredients but the garnishes vigorously with ice. Strain into a
highball glass filled with ice, and garnish with the lime wedge and chile slice.

1 1/2 ounces silver tequila
1/2 ounce Cointreau
1 ounce fresh lime juice
1/2 ounce fresh lemon juice
1/4 cup diced ripe avocado
1 ounce half-and-half
1 teaspoon sugar
Lime wedge
Avocado slice

Combine all ingredients but the garnishes in a blender with 1/2 cup ice. Blend 
until smooth. Pour into a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with the lime and 
avocado.

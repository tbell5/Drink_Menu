Frozen Matador:

2 ounces gold tequila
1/4 ounce triple sec
1/4 cup fresh pineapple chunks
1/2 ounce fresh lime juice

Variation; Combine the ingredients in a blender with 1/2 cup crushed ice, and 
blend until smooth. Pour into a chilled highball glass.

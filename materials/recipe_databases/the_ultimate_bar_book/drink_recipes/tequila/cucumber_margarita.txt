1 tablespoon superfine sugar
1 ounce fresh lime juice
1/4 cup thinly sliced English cucumber
1 1/2 ounces silve tequila
1 ounce Cointreau

Put the sugar and lime juice in the bottom of an old-fashioned glass. Add the
cucumber and muddle. Add the tequila and Cointreau. Fill the glass with ice and
stir.

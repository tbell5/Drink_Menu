2 ounces gold tequila
1/2 ounce apricot brandy
1 1/2 ounces fresh lime juice
3 ounces fresh grapefruit juice
1/4 ounce maraschino cherry juice (from a jar of cherries)
Lime wedge

Shake the liquid ingredients vigorously with ice. Strain into an ice-filled 
highball glass. Squeeze the lime wedge over the drink, and drop it in.

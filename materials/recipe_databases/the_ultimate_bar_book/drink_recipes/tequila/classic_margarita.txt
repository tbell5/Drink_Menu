2 lime wedges
kosher salt
1 1/2 ounces premium silver tequila
1 1/2 ounces fresh lime juice
1 ounce Cointreau

Rub a wedge of lime around the rime of a chilled margarita glass, and salt the
rim. Fill the prepared glass with ice. Shake the liquid ingredients 
vigorously with ice. Strain into the prepared glass. Squeeze the remaining lime 
wedge over the drink, and drop it in.

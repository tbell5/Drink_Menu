2 ounces tequila
1/2 ounce Cointreau
1/2 ounce strawberry liqueur
1/2 ounce fresh lime juice
2 to 3 sliced, hulled fresh strawberries
1 strawberry
1 lime wheel

Blend all ingredients except the garnish with 1 cup crushed ice until smooth. 
Pour into a chilled margarita glass. Garnish with the strawberry and lime wheel.
Variations: see Peach Margarita; Mango Margarita; Melon Margarita

1 1/2 ounces vanilla-infused tequila
3/4 ounce Citronge (or other orange liqueur)
2 ounces fresh tangerine juice
1 ounce sweet-and-sour
3 or 4 fresh mint leaves
Tangerine Wheel
Mint Sprig

Shake the liquid ingredients and mint leaves vigorously with ice. Strain into
a chilled cocktail glass. Garnish with the tangerine wheel and mint sprig.
